So, you'd like to know more about me? I'm a writer, front end web developer and adventure seeker from Seattle. I'm also a professional Irish dancer - true story - and emerging techie. I have a degree in Sociology from The University of British Columbia, and love to communicate with clients about how to make their projects accessable, enjoyable and clear.

The explorer in me has given me countless opportunities that contribute to my experiences as a writer, deigner and human. I've worked as a Park Ranger, Kayak Guide, Dancer, Camp Counselor and Pedi-Cab Driver. I've managed small and large teams in multiple settings and worked as a freelancer, artist in residence and in several school districts. I've lived in Yellowstone National Park, Ketchikan, Alaska, Vancouver, British Columbia and, of course, Seattle. I love to work with small business owners, artists and creatives to build content and web design that fits their personal needs.

Find out more about my writing, or check out some of my web design. I am available for different types of projects, but love combining my copy writing experience with my skills in webdesign, which could give you a fully functional website. Interested in getting to know me even better? Read my blog, where I write about all sorts of things, but mostly about food.
